RaDaR

Welcome to the homepage of the UK Registry for Rare Kidney Diseases (RaDaR).

RaDaR is where healthcare professionals register patients and enter data.

Go to RareRenal.org for a list of diagnoses and check that your patient is eligible for the registry.

If you need patient information sheets and consent forms you can find them here.

You can then log-in to RaDaR as a RaDaR/RPV Administrator. If this is your first visit you will need

to register as a user.
